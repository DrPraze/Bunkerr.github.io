About Bunkerr

What is it?

Bunkerr is a networking security startup founded by Praise James

Bunkerr offers brilliant Cyber Security and Networking Solutions.

The main ‘thrust’ is to focus on making the world comfortable with it's cyber security matters in good hands. Eliminating serious cyber vulnerabilities, whilst ensuring secured communication and utilisation of their data, thus promoting secure and better networking technology. It also engages in cyber security educating to increase awareness of cyber security threats and how to deal with them.
